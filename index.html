🐾 A Story of Hope

I’m turning 18 soon, and I live in Uganda. Every day, I see cats on the streets—hungry, injured, and struggling to survive. Some are abandoned, some are born into harsh conditions, and many don’t make it without help. I couldn’t just walk past anymore. What started as helping one cat turned into something much bigger. I began rescuing them from the streets, giving them food, a safe place to stay, and the medical care they desperately need. Each cat has a story—some have been hurt, some are sick, and some have never known what it feels like to be safe. This small effort has grown into a shelter—a place where these cats can finally rest, recover, and have a chance at a better life. But I can’t do this alone. Food, treatment, and shelter all require resources, and the number of cats in need keeps growing. Every bit of support makes a real difference—whether it’s helping feed them, providing medical care, or giving them a safe place to stay. Together, we can give these animals a second chance. Please consider donating to help feed and care for these cats. Your support can save lives and bring hope to those who need it most. Every penny matters.
